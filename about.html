Let's get to know me better

I am Oleksandra Kniazieva, a sophomore majoring in Contemporary Communication.

I have worked as social media manager for two years in social activism and contemporary art. While creating and developing a marketing and content plan for my clients, I implement a client-first approach, Integrated marketing communication (IMC), and help clients build a Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

I profoundly love kids and animals. If I had to choose, what to do till my last day, I would stick to helping and developing a safe environment for those two groups. Moreover, I have been vegan for four years; hence, running social media for animal protective organizations is exciting and relatable. Explore my mission and vision.
